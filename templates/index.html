Patient Blood Management System

Keck School of Medicine of USC

What we do?

Patient Blood Management System,to address needs of patients, as well as improving business processes and revenue (ex: cancelled surgeries, cost of blood products).The system predicts the amount of blood products required in a surgery, and also fills missing patient data based on the limited dataset available from Keck School.
  • The fields predicted are: Red Blood Cells, Fresh Frozen Plasma, Platelets, Cryoprecipitate and Allogenic (Yes/No).
  • The missing fields filled are: Age, Duration of Surgery, SN-BM-Pre-Op INR, SN-BM-Pre-Op Platelet Count, SN-BM-PRBC Ordered, Allogeneic Blood Transfusion, SN-BM- Red Blood Cells, SN -BM- Fresh Frozen Plasma, SN-BM - Platelets, SN - BM -Cryoprecipitate, ResultsBeforeSurgery.

What is Train?

Whenever there are hundreds of new patients records, and it has been a while, since the old metrics to train have been used, Hit the Train button.

How to Train?

Upload a file to Train:
Please enter a CSV file with the below columns in same order:
Masked FIN, Age, Sequence No., SURG_Procedure, Duration of Surgery(hh:mm), SURGICAL_SPECIALITY, Surgeon Hash Name, PATIENT_TYPE, SN-BM-Pre-Op INR, SN-BM -Pre-Op Platelet Count, SN-BM-PRBC Ordered, Allogenic Blood Transfusion, SN-BM-Red Blood Cells, SN-BM- Fresh Frozen Plasma, SN-BM- Platelets, SN-BM-Cryoprecipitate, ResultsBeforeSurgery, ResultAfterSurgery, EBL

Fill missing records - Check this checkbox to trigger the Recommendation Engine module to fill the missing data, if any, in the training data. This generates a csv file that gets downloaded to the temp directory and be referred any time.

Click on Train button to fire the Prediction module to learn on the patient data fed.

What is Predict?

To get the predicted amount of blood to be pre-ordered before a surgery, Use Predict.

How to Predict?

Upload patient record in the form and hit Predict. For multiple patients the system generates CSV with predicted values for SN-BM-Red Blood Cells, SN-BM-Fresh Frozen Plasma, SN-BM-Platelets, SN-BM- Cryoprecipitate, Allogenic (Yes/ No).
Based on these values, it can be decided how much and what type of blood to be preordered. The amount predicted is accurate 78 out of 100 times.
